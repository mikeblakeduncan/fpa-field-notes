AI in Finance: The Order of Operations—Why Tools Aren't Enough

by Subha Kumar CA

Subha Kumar argues that finance teams struggle with AI not because of tool gaps, but because they lack a strategic order of operations. He outlines a five-step framework: warm up with education, eliminate waste before automating, identify quick wins, document with TRACE criteria (Traceable, Repeatable, Auditable, Compliant, Explainable), and limit tools to 2-3 to avoid 'AI brain fry'.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN → Before automating anything, map your workflows, kill the garbage work, and build a TRACE-compliant implementation strategy that your CFO can understand in 60 seconds.
